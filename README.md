# Hospital_Emergency_Room_Analysis

Project Overview

This project focuses on analysing hospital emergency room operations using interactive dashboards developed in Microsoft Excel with Power Query, Power Pivot, DAX, Pivot Tables, and advanced dashboarding techniques.
The objective of this project was to transform raw healthcare data into meaningful operational insights that could help hospital management improve decision-making, monitor patient trends, optimise resource allocation, and enhance overall patient experience.


Key Insights Derived


The average patient satisfaction score remained consistently high at 4.99/5 across 1.8 years of data.

Approximately 41% of patients were attended within 30 minutes, while the overall average wait time was 35 minutes.

The 20–29 age group recorded the highest emergency room visits.

A large number of patients were referred to the Orthopedic Department, indicating potential trends related to physical injuries or trauma related cases.

Around 49% of patients were not admitted, suggesting a high proportion of non-critical treatments and routine emergency visits.

Male patients accounted for approximately 51% of total patient visits.
